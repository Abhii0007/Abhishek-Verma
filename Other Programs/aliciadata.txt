Question:-   how to make masala dosa

Answers:-


Ingredients: 
-3 cups idli/dosa batter 
-2 onions, chopped 
-2 green chillies, chopped 
-1/4 cup fresh coriander, chopped 
-1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
-1/4 teaspoon cumin powder 
-1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder 
-Oil/ghee, to grease the pan
-Salt, to taste 

Instructions: 
1. In a bowl, mix together chopped onions, green chillies, coriander, pepper powder, cumin powder, and turmeric powder. 
2. Take a non-stick griddle/tawa and grease it with some oil/ghee. 
3. Pour 1/2 cup of idli/dosa batter into the center of the griddle and spread it in a thin and even layer. 
4. Drizzle 1 teaspoon of oil/ghee around the edges of the dosa. 
5. Sprinkle generous amount of prepared masala over the dosa. 
6. Cook for a few minutes until the base is light golden brown. 
7. Fold the dosa into half and serve hot with chutney or sambar.

Question:-   what is ic

Answers:-

Integrated circuit (IC), also referred to as a microchip, is a small electronic device made up of a semiconductor material (usually silicon) and other electronic components. These devices are used in almost every electronic device today, including computers, mobile phones, radios, televisions, and more. ICs are made up of very tiny transistors, resistors, capacitors, and other components, which form the basic building blocks of many electronic designs. An IC can be a single component or an integrated circuit board comprising of many components.

Question:-   write python code for finding prime numbers

Answers:-



def is_prime(n):
    if n < 2:
        return False
    for i in range(2, n):
        if n % i == 0:
            return False
    return True

for i in range(2, 100):
    if is_prime(i):
        print(i)
